# RAKSO Company Foundation

## Mission
Identify, structure and automate operational bottlenecks that consume time, money and attention inside small and medium businesses.

## What RAKSO Is
A company focused on AI-powered business operations, workflow automation and operational intelligence.

## What RAKSO Is Not
- Not a tax company
- Not a chatbot company
- Not an AI agency dependent on prompts
- Not a single-product company

## Core Thesis
We do not sell AI.
We do not sell agents.
We solve operational bottlenecks.

## Foundation Layers
1. Company
2. Framework (SSD+, Fortress OS, Governance)
3. Operational Capabilities
4. Vertical Solutions

## Phase 1 Cashflow Initiatives
### TaxPak Phase 1
Client Readiness Engine for tax preparers.

### Bookkeeping AI Assistant
Business Readiness Engine for small businesses and bookkeepers.

## Future Expansion
Small Business Solutions:
- FrontDesk AI
- Lead Follow-Up

- Social Media Operations

Products may change.
