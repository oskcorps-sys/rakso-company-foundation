# RAKSO Principles

## 1. Solve Bottlenecks, Not Technology Problems
RAKSO focuses on operational friction, not on selling AI for its own sake.

## 2. Human In The Loop
Humans remain accountable for critical business, financial, legal, compliance and customer-impact decisions.

## 3. Simplicity Before Complexity
Validate manually, automate second, scale third.

## 4. ROI First
Every solution must recover time, money, capacity, accuracy or attention.

## 5. Reusable Capabilities
Build capabilities that can be reused across industries instead of isolated one-off projects.

## 6. Governance By Design
Observability, accountability, traceability and permissions are part of the system from the beginning.

## 7. Cashflow Funds Innovation
Revenue-generating solutions come before platform expansion.

## 8. Vertical Solutions Are Vehicles
TaxPak, Bookkeeping AI and future packs are validation and cashflow vehicles. They are not the company itself.

## 9. Technology Is Replaceable
Models, tools and vendors can change. The process, method and operational insight must survive.

## 10. Do Not Overbuild The Future
Build the smallest reliable version that solves today's painful workflow and creates evidence for the next step.
