# RAKSO Business Model

## Core Business
RAKSO identifies, structures and automates operational bottlenecks for SMBs and professional service businesses.

## Revenue Strategy

### Phase 1
Productized Solutions
- TaxPak Phase 1
- Bookkeeping AI Assistant

Goal: Generate cashflow and gather operational data.

### Phase 2
Capability Expansion
- FrontDesk AI
- Lead Follow-Up
- Reviews Automation
- Scheduling Automation

### Phase 3
Operational Platform
Reusable capabilities shared across industries.

## Commercial Model
- Monthly subscription
- Setup fee when required
- Managed automation retainers
- Premium implementation projects

## Success Metric
Hours recovered, operational capacity created and measurable business outcomes.
