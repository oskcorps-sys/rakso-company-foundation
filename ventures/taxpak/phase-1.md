# TaxPak Phase 1

## Objective
Client Readiness Engine for Tax Preparers.

## Problem
Tax preparers lose time chasing documents, tracking missing information and managing incomplete cases.

## MVP Scope
- Client portal
- Dynamic checklist
- Document upload
- Status tracking
- Automated reminders
- Case readiness dashboard

## Success Metric
Time required to move a client from onboarding to ready-for-review.

## Not Included
- Tax preparation
- E-file
- Tax advice
- Automated filing
- Advanced OCR processing

## Goal
Generate first paying customers and validate operational pain points before expanding into document intelligence.
