# Bookkeeping AI Assistant Phase 1

## Objective
Business Readiness Engine for small businesses.

## Problem
Small businesses often maintain incomplete, inaccurate or outdated books, creating extra work for bookkeepers and tax preparers.

## MVP Scope
- QuickBooks connectivity
- Readiness assessment
- Missing information detection
- Checklist generation
- Follow-up automation

## Success Metric
Reduction in cleanup and preparation time before bookkeeping or tax work begins.

## Not Included
- Full bookkeeping services
- Accounting replacement
- Autonomous financial decisions

## Goal
Validate recurring year-round demand and create a second cashflow engine for RAKSO.
